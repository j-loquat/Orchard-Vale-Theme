III. Infrastructure Overview

A. Roads & Bridges

The road network of Orchard Vale is the lifeline of our rural economy, connecting farms, forges, and markets across a diverse landscape. Over the past decade, steady investments in maintenance and bridge restoration have improved reliability, though seasonal wear and funding gaps remain persistent challenges.

"

A well-kept road does more than move goods, it moves opportunity.

Key Takeaway

Bridge restorations deliver the highest reliability impact per gold invested.

Bridges remain the costliest assets to maintain, yet their failure risk has decreased notably since the 18,020 flood season. Preventative upkeep proves far less costly than emergency reconstruction.
